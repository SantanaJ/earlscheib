ABOUT US:
Earl Scheib of Southern California is a chain of 3 paint and auto body shops conveniently located throughout the Los Angeles area for all of your auto body, collision, restoration, and painting needs. We provide all the services you need to get your car looking as good as new, whether it needs a full restoration or just some simple dent repair. If you’re making a claim through your insurance company you’ll be glad to know that we work with most major insurance carriers and we'll make the process as smooth as possible. Great paint, great service at a great price! Earl Scheib Paint & Body has been around since 1937 and our years of experience will ensure a fast, efficient, professional job for your paint and dent repair needs. Need an oversized vehicle painted? Our Montclair location can handle buses, semis, trailers, and other large jobs. Feel free to call or e-mail any of our locations for pricing and more information, and we'll be more than happy to get your car looking as good as new.
